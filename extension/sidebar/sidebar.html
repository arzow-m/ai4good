About Perspect

Perspect uses AI to detect persuasive and manipulative language techniques in real time as you browse. It highlights flagged phrases, explains the technique being used, and helps you read more critically.

Version 1.0.0 perspect.ai
Data & Privacy

Text from posts you scroll past is sent to our analysis server to detect persuasion techniques. Post text is not stored or logged. Chat messages are processed by a third-party LLM (Groq) and are subject to their privacy policy. We do not collect personal data or browsing history.
